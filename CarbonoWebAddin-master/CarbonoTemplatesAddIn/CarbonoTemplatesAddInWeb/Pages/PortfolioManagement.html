The Carbono add-in turns management of medium-sized and large portfolios – such as project portfolios or investment stakes – more effective.

It allows you to 1) create working templates – or use the free ready-to-use project portfolio file above, 2) distribute them to the respective persons responsible, and 3) consolidate all inserted data and information in a summarizing cockpit.
